About Us

The persistent problem of Interview collaboration has been a challenge that has long been unaddressed . With every company facing the challenge of aligning technical expertise with potential candidates and consecutive steps, the hiring process has not only become cumbersome but time-consuming.

The Conception

Over the years, playing roles of Technology Executives in Silicon Valley companies, the Founders behind Qualifyde have observed lack of maturity, lack of quality processes and solutions in the area of technical assessments and in interview collobration. To address this problem they joined hands to find a solution to these business and revenue impacting problems.

After multiple brainstorming and with inputs from end users and industry experts, Qualifyde an IAAS (Interview As A Solution) Platform was born which helps Hiring Managers streamline hiring processes and candidates assessment much faster and with efficiency.

Qualifyde platform enables collaboration between hiring managers, freshers, career growth aspiring individual and Technical Experts. It enables faster technical assessments, reduces significantly internal resources usage, improves quality of hiring for hiring managers and helps increase revenue and credibility for Staffing companies.

The purpose

Today Qualifyde aims to reduce the hiring cycles by more than 30% by streamlining the entire process. Creating a live Interview platform where Hiring Managers, Recruiters, Staffing Companies can easily choose our Tech Gurus to conduct Interviews, get unbiased feedback with great detail and with short turn-around time. Qualifyde is a perfect AI based solution, with on-demand Interviews and a live candidate dashboard - An all-in-all hiring solution that helps you navigate swiftly through the interviewing and hiring process.

Work life balance is not just a trend anymore - It is practiced at Qualifyde.
